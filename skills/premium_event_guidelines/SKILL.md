# Premium Event Planning Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines when creating plans:
- **Luxury Events:** For luxury events or premium weddings, ensure that at least 35% of the total budget is allocated to the Venue & Setup.
- **Weather Mitigation:** If the event has any outdoor components, suggest a tent or marquee as a primary weather mitigation plan in the Risk Assessment section.
- **Corporate Tech:** Corporate conferences must prioritize high-speed Wi-Fi and premium AV production setups in their checklists.
